Elementary School

Moon Mountain Elementary

The first ever school I attended was Moon Mountain Elementary, starting my school years with Head Start. The first day of Head Start was quite a scary day for me at the time, mainly for the reason that it was the first time my mom wasn't with me during that time. During kindergarten I made some new friends which are still my friends even to this day. While at Moon Mountain I discovered the passion I had for art. My mom then bought me a set of colored pencils and a sketch book for me to draw in which I still have to this day. Overall elementary school was nothing too special for me.

Middle School

Mountain Sky Junior High

The middle school that I went to was Mountain Sky and I personally disliked it. Although class started at 8am which was nice to have since previously for elementary, school started at 7am. It was the first time that I met other student from different schools which I made friends with. It was also the first time I had different class periods. During the two years at this school I had two different science teachers that were very different than the others. They really pushed me to do more than what I thought I could. Looking forward to science was really the only thing I looked forward to other than getting to see my friends.

High School

Thunderbird High School

At the time of me writing this I am still attending Thunderbird High School and am a Junior. My Freshman year was a pretty good year for me, luckily high school wasn't what many told me it would be like. I did make some new friends during my Freshman year which I still have to this day. My Sophomore year was still a pretty easy year for me, I didn't have any difficult classes other than Honors Spanish because writing was and still is difficult for me. All the other classes I had were pretty easy. Now that I am in my Junior year, it is going pretty well. Again none of my classes are challenging, but some do assign a bit too much work which I believe is unnecessary. Overall my high school experience so far is going great, I neither like or dislike it.
